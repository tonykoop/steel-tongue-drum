# Image-Gen Prompts

Concept image only; not fabrication authority. Do not include cut-ready dimensions, hole locations, or toolpaths.

## Hero Prompt

A finished circular steel tongue drum photographed as a real, hand-built idiophone: a round low-profile steel shell, roughly the proportions of a small drum that fits in two hands, with a domed or gently rounded top plate cut with a radial fan of eleven slit tongues of graduated length splaying out from the center toward the rim. The tongues read clearly as cut slots in the steel surface, each terminating in a smooth rounded slot-end, the longest bass tongue prominent and the shorter high tongues stepping down in size around the field. Brushed low-carbon steel with a subtle satin clear finish, faint mill grain catching the light, softly radiused rim, no welds or seams emphasized. Three rubber feet barely visible at the base lift the body slightly off the surface. Realistic tabletop scale cues, a soft-tipped mallet resting beside it. Clean neutral studio background, soft directional studio light raking across the top to reveal the tongue slots, 3/4 product-photography angle, ~50mm lens, high detail, photorealistic, no text labels.

## In-Context Prompt

A steel tongue drum staged on a worn wooden workbench in a metal-fabrication workshop, soft natural window light, a pair of soft mallets and a small handful of deburring files nearby, faint steel dust and a coupon test-cut offcut in the background telling the story of an empirical, tuned-by-hand instrument. A hand hovers over the longest tongue mid-strike. Warm raking daylight catches the brushed steel top and the radial slit pattern, shallow depth of field with the tongue field in sharp focus and the shop softly blurred behind, environmental storytelling, concept visualization only.

## Detail Prompt

Macro close-up of the signature mechanism: the root of a single cut steel tongue where the slit terminates in a smooth radiused slot-end, the cut kerf running cleanly through brushed steel, a faint deburred chamfer along the slot edge catching the light. Show the satin tooled texture of the steel, the subtle warmth of a clear protective finish, and the gentle curvature of the top plate. Raking light grazes the slot to reveal depth and edge quality, high detail, no measurements.

## Negative / Avoid

No text/logos/watermarks/fake maker marks, no dimension callouts, no impossible mechanisms; anatomically plausible hands if a player is shown.
