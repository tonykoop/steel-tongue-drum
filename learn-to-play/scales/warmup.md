# Steel Tongue Drum Warm-Up

## Scale walk (D natural minor)
Ascending:
```
X:1
T:D Natural Minor Scale Walk
M:4/4
L:1/4
Q:1/4=72
K:Dmin
D E F G | A B c d | d c B A | G F E D |
```
*(In K:Dmin, b = Bb automatically)*

## Pentatonic core (D F G A C)
The 5-note subset that covers most pentatonic-friendly melodies:
```
X:2
T:D Minor Pentatonic
M:4/4
L:1/4
K:Dmin
D F G A | c d c A | G F D2 |
```

## Drone improvisation
Hold the bass tongue (D3) as a drone and play freely over the other notes.
The D drone creates a meditative, sustained base for any notes you choose.
