# Notes: Ode to Joy (Steel Tongue Drum Adaptation)

**Provenance:** Beethoven's melody (1824), PD. This is a free adaptation into D Aeolian —
not a transcription of any copyrighted modern arrangement.

**Range check:** A3–A4. Uses the heart of the drum (tongues 1 through 8).

**Practice tips:**
1. The dotted rhythm (f3 e) = dotted quarter + eighth — count "1-and-2-AND" 
2. The adaptation into D minor gives a more contemplative feel than the triumphant original.
3. Use the drone D3 bass tongue between phrases for extra resonance.
