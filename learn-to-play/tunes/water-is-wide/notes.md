# Notes: The Water Is Wide

**Provenance:** Traditional English/Scottish ballad. PD. 
Arranged here for D natural minor — the original is commonly played in G or F major.
No copyrighted arrangement is reproduced; melody is freshly arranged.

**Range check:** E4–D5. Upper D5 appears at the emotional peak of phrase 2.

**Practice tips:**
1. The pickup note A starts before bar 1 — a soft, gentle entrance.
2. The Bb (b in K:Dmin) lends the arrangement a folk-minor quality.
3. Play at ♩= 72 if ♩= 80 feels rushed — this tune benefits from spaciousness.
