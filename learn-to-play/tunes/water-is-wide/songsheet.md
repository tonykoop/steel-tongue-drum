# The Water Is Wide — Songsheet (Steel Tongue Drum)

**Key:** D natural minor (F major equivalent)  **Tempo:** ♩= 80  
**Meter:** 4/4  **Difficulty:** ★★☆  **Range:** E4–D5  
**Source:** Traditional, PD; arranged for D Aeolian

## Music
```
X:4 T:The Water Is Wide M:4/4 L:1/4 Q:1/4=80 K:Dmin
A | A3/2 c/ A F | G3 E | F G A B | c4 |
c3/2 c/ c A | c d c A | G3/2 A/ G F | A4 |
```
*(b = Bb; d = D5)*

## Notes
- The graceful ascending and descending phrases match the steel tongue drum's blooming resonance.
- The Bb (b in K:Dmin) gives the folk color — lean into it slightly.
- This is the most "singing" tune in the set — play with feeling and slight rubato.
