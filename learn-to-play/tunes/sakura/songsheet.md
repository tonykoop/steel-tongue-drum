# Sakura Sakura — Songsheet

**Key:** A Bb D E (In scale / Sakura pentatonic)  **Tempo:** ♩= 72
**Meter:** 4/4  **Difficulty:** ★☆☆  **Range:** A3–E4
**Source:** Traditional Japanese, Edo period (18th c.), public domain

## Music
```
X:1 T:Sakura Sakura M:4/4 L:1/4 Q:1/4=72 K:Dmin
A B d2 | A B d2 | A B d e | d B A2 |
A B d e | d B A2 | A d d B | A4 |
```
*(In K:Dmin, b = Bb. Use notes A3, Bb3, D4, E4 only.)*

## Notes
- Uses only 4 notes from your drum: **A, Bb, D, E** — the "In" (Sakura) pentatonic.
- The slow 4/4 feel should be meditative — leave space after each phrase.
- The bass D tongue can serve as a drone between phrases.

**Provenance:** Traditional Japanese koto melody, Edo period. PD worldwide.
