# Simple Gifts — Songsheet (Steel Tongue Drum)

**Key:** F major  **Tempo:** ♩= 88  **Meter:** 4/4  **Difficulty:** ★★☆  
**Range:** E4–D5  **Source:** Elder Joseph Brackett, Shaker hymn (1848), PD

*(F major: Bb is automatically b in K:F. Your drum has Bb3 — play it for the b note.)*

## Notes
- F major uses all the same notes as D natural minor (they share a key signature).
- The melody moves in smooth arches — connect notes fluidly.
- D5 (d') at the end of bar 4 is the peak — strike it confidently and let it ring.
