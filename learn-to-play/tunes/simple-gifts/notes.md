# Notes: Simple Gifts (Steel Tongue Drum)

**Provenance:** Elder Joseph Brackett, Shaker hymn (1848). PD. 
Copland's arrangement is NOT used — freshly arranged from the lead-sheet melody.

**Range check:** E4–D5. E4 is the lowest note (tongue 5), D5 is the highest (tongue 10/11).

**Practice tips:**
1. F major and D natural minor share the same notes (relative modes) — your drum was built for this.
2. The tied final note (f8-) should ring uninterrupted for 4 full beats.
3. The "arch" gesture (ascending then descending) is the structural key — practice it at ♩= 60 first.
