# Notes: Dusk Bloom

**Composition:** Original tune for this steel tongue drum songbook.
MIT licensed — part of the Heifer Zephyr collection.
Written for a D natural minor (Aeolian) steel tongue drum with 11 tongues.

**Range check:** D3–E4. Uses the full drum, including the bass D3 tongue.

**Design notes:**
- Built around D minor pentatonic (D F G A C) with the full E4 added for color.
- The bass D (D3) serves as a structural anchor for the outer phrases.
- Phrase lengths: 4 bars each × 4 phrases = 16 bars total; forms a complete arch.
