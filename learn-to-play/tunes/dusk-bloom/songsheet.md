# Dusk Bloom — Songsheet

**Key:** D Aeolian (natural minor)  **Tempo:** ♩= 80  **Meter:** 4/4  **Difficulty:** ★★★  
**Range:** D3–E4  **License:** MIT (original composition — Heifer Zephyr)

## Music
```
X:5 T:Dusk Bloom M:4/4 L:1/8 Q:1/4=80 K:Dmin
D4 A4 | d4 c4 | A4 G4 | F8 |
F4 A4 | c4 d4 | c4 A4 | G8 |
A4 c4 | d4 e4 | d4 c4 | A8 |
G4 F4 | G4 A4 | d4 c4 | D8 |
```
*(D3 bass is the opening note; use the bass tongue to anchor each phrase)*

## Character
A slow, meditative piece that uses the full range of the drum — from the bass D3
tongue up to E4. The arching phrases evoke a gradual descent from light to shadow.
Each sustained note (half notes and whole notes) should ring fully into the next.

## Notes
- Begin with the bass D3 tongue — this is the drum's foundation note.
- The E4 in bar 10 is the only departure from the D minor pentatonic core — it provides
  the harmonic "color" of the natural minor scale.
- Play with closed eyes if possible; let the overtones of each tongue guide the next note.
