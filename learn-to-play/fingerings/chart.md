## Striking tips
- Center of each tongue produces clearest fundamental
- Tip of tongue produces shorter, higher-pitched tone
- Base of tongue near slit produces more fundamental
- Softer mallets: more bloom, longer sustain
- Harder mallets: faster attack, cleaner articulation
